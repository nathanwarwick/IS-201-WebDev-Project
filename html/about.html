Me :)

I was born in Orem, UT and raised in Illinois in a city called Naperville I absolutely loved it there. When I was younger, I watched a professional broadway show called "Wicked." After watching that, I knew I loved the arts. I played the Oboe in high school for the band and sometimes the orchestra, wrote, played in, and directed shows, and participated in volleyball. I loved my time in high school. In 2018, I had the opportunity to serve a mission in the Philippines (flag pictured above) and I absolutely loved it with all of my heart. My favorite companion is pictured down below as well as a picture I took above of one of my areas in the picture for "About Me."


In 2020, I came home from my mission due to Covid. I served a total of 21 months and they were some of the best months of my life. i grew tons of great relationships and forever bonds with the people out there. I now live in Utah where I go to school and I have made the best with my situation. I have gained many friends, gone on tons of adventures, made mistakes, and will do so much more. I am happy to be living this life.
